This is called the Lattice Method. It is used to solve multiplication questions. What we have to do, is we need to multiply the numbers grid by grid
Step 1:
Lattice is simple!
Let's do it together. In the first grid,
5 x 9 = 45
So we add in 4 and 5 in the box like so.
Step 2:
The next grid,
6 x 9 = 54.
So we add 5 and 4 into the boxes.
Step 3:
Same steps as step 2 and step 3
Fill in the remaining boxes.
Step 4:
Add the numbers diagonally from the bottom right to the top left.
8 = 8
4 + 4 + 0 = 8
5 + 5 + 4 = 14
If the number exceeds 9, carry the 1 forward to the next diagonal
